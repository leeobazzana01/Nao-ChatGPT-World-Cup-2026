# Knowledge Base — NAO Robot

## About the Robot

The NAO robot is a humanoid developed by SoftBank Robotics.
It is 58 cm tall and weighs approximately 5.4 kg.
NAO has 25 degrees of freedom, allowing natural gestures and movements.

## Capabilities

NAO can speak in more than 20 different languages.
It has HD cameras for computer vision and facial recognition.
The robot has 4 microphones for 360-degree audio capture.
NAO can detect and track human faces using computer vision.
It has touch sensors on its head, hands, and feet.
NAO can stand up on its own after falling.

## How to Interact

To start a conversation, speak clearly after the beep.
When you finish speaking, wait — the robot will say "I am thinking" while it processes.
To end the conversation, say "no" or "stop".
The robot captures a photo before responding for better visual context.
